Kolkata India

Went to Kolkata in April 2016 as a part of HOPE's fundraising programme.

I raised over 3000 Euro for the children of Kolkata.

Although Kolkata is a poverty striken City, it is still undoubtedly one of the most interesting Cities i have or ever will visit.

One street with shanty houses and the next street with designer shops.

The contrast between poor and rich is what makes this city so facinating.

Seeing things such as people living on the train tracks to going to a million euor temple in which they all pray is truly spectacular.

After college i plan to fundraise more and visit and voluntere in Kolkata for an entire summer.
