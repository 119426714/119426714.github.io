Sports

Hockey

My love for hockey began when I was 8 years old.

The primary school i attended was big into hockey.

As a girl who loved sports i was determined to be on the first team.

We trained every week and soon i was on the first team playing midfeild.

We competed in the Cork Hockey Cup and won. .

My hockey career continued in secondary school.

I was on the junior and senior team throughout my time in Scoil Mhuire.

Training was weekly and it was a great way to make new friends.

We won the Junior Munster Hockey Cup in 2015.

Unfortunetly we lost the Senior Munster Hockey Cup to MMC 2019.

Swimming

I started swimming when I was 3 years old in Silver Springs Cork.

With lessons every week soon I was a confident, strong swimmer.

My swimming instructor was tough and sometimes too tough.

I moved to Aqua Leisure in Youghal.

I continued to swim in the Maradyke.

I do swimming camps every summerin Ardmore.

I compete in many competions around Ireland.

Competions are a great way to make new friends.
